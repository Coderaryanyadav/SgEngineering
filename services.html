Cranes & Lifting Services

Full-cycle solutions: Concept to Compliance.

1. Custom Design & Manufacturing

We provide tailored crane systems for site-specific needs. Our design team uses advanced computerized data banks for structural and mechanical analysis to ensure optimal performance and safety.

2. Erection & Commissioning

Our experienced technicians ensure efficient fabrication, erection, and installation. We verify everything against developed operation and maintenance manuals for smooth functioning.

3. Retrofit & Revamp

Modernize your old cranes (Refurbishment of EOT Cranes). We upgrade aging systems with new technology and safety features, extending their operational life and improving compliance.

4. Annual Maintenance Contracts (AMC)

We offer comprehensive AMC Shield programs. This includes scheduled servicing, emergency support, overhauling, and health checkups of cranes to prevent unplanned downtime.

Specialized Load Testing service

We perform rigorous load testing for Double Girder EOT and Semi-Goliath Cranes.

  • Safe Working Load (SWL) verification.
  • Tested @ 13.5T for 10T cranes (all motions: Hoist, Cross Travel & Long Travel).
  • Structural stability, Brake, and Drive setting checks.
  • Verification of safety features: Rotary, Gravity & Two-way Lever Limit switches.
  • Load Cell Calibration with indicator (accuracy of 1% Tolerance).
